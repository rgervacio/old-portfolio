About

Hello, my name is Rachelle.

I’m a technical writer and editor based in Winnipeg, Manitoba.

With a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology (University of Winnipeg) and a Certificate in Technical Communication (Simon Fraser University), I specialize in creating end-user guides and training materials. As a user advocate, I write with the intentions to understand end-user perspectives and teach through simple and clear information.

My interest in technical writing began rather unexpectedly. While working as a writing tutor, I enjoyed finding ways to improve written documents and mentoring students to become more efficient and effective writers. I found it fulfilling to break down complex information into straightforward concepts and terms. Additionally, in 2019, I had the privilege to co-author an article that was published in the Journal of Chemical Education. During this experience, I discovered a fascination with technical communication and how developing practical instructional resources can improve learning experiences, outcomes, and design.

To cut a long story short, I realized that I wanted to create informational and instructional content that could help people achieve their goals, no matter how big or small.

During my spare time, I like to draw, listen to podcasts, and practice cardistry flourishes. I’ve also developed an interest in beekeeping, and I hope to have my own backyard apiary one day.

Services

Here is how I can create and improve your communications.

Audience Research

To create relevant and engaging content that produces results, I apply various research methods to identify your audience's specific needs and goals.

Examples:

  • Subject-matter expert (SME) interviews
  • Audience and task analyses
  • User personas
  • Usability testing

User Documentation

Whether your users are experts or beginners, I create print and electronic deliverables that are easily accessible, logically organized, and visually appealing.

Examples:

  • Guides and manuals
  • Technical and business reports
  • Screencasts and video tutorials
  • E-learning modules

Editing & Proofreading

I ensure that your documentation is clear, concise, and accurate by editing for coherent structure, appropriate tone and style, and correct writing mechanics.

Examples:

  • Revising
  • Substantive editing
  • Copy editing
  • Proofreading
